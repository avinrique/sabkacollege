About Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering

Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering (DSCE) is a leading institution for technical education in India, established in 1979 under the Mahatma Gandhi Vidya Peetha Trust. Approved by AICTE and affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, the college is part of the renowned Dayananda Sagar Institutions (DSI) group, one of the largest educational conglomerates in Karnataka.

Located on a 7-acre campus in Kumaraswamy Layout, Bangalore, DSCE holds the prestigious NAAC A++ accreditation and autonomous status, with several programs accredited by NBA. The institution has 18 research centers dedicated to advanced research and Ph.D. studies under VTU.

DSCE offers 20 undergraduate (UG) programs and 6 postgraduate (PG) programs across diverse engineering, technology, and management disciplines, including emerging fields like Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Robotics & AI, and Computer Science & Design. The institution is recognized among the top 20 engineering colleges in Bangalore and has a strong track record of placements with 150+ companies visiting the campus annually.

The college has strong industry collaborations with companies like Bosch, Dell, and TCS, along with dedicated R&D centers and innovation labs. DSCE also offers postgraduate programs including M.Tech, MBA, MCA, and Ph.D., making it a comprehensive institution for higher education in engineering and management.

Eligibility Criteria & Entrance Exams

B.E. Programs

  • Academic: Minimum 45% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry & Mathematics (PCM) in 10+2 or equivalent (40% for SC/ST categories)
  • Entrance Exams: KCET (Karnataka CET) or COMEDK UGET
  • Management Quota: Direct admission based on 10+2 marks and merit

M.Tech Programs

  • Academic: B.E./B.Tech with minimum 50% aggregate from a recognized university
  • Entrance Exams: PGCET / GATE / KMT / CMAT / MAT

MBA Program

  • Academic: 50% aggregate in graduation from a recognized university
  • Entrance Exams: PGCET / KMAT / CMAT / MAT

MCA Program

  • Academic: Bachelor's degree with minimum 50% aggregate (45% for SC/ST categories)
  • Entrance Exams: PGCET / KMT

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the most popular courses at DSCE Bangalore?
The most popular courses at DSCE are B.E. in Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Information Science & Engineering (ISE), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI&ML), and Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE). CSE has the highest intake of 180 seats.
What is the fee structure at DSCE Bangalore?
DSCE fees vary by admission category. KCET students pay approximately ₹1,05,000 per year and COMEDK students pay approximately ₹2,45,000 per year for B.E. programs. Management quota fees may differ. Contact SabkaCollege at +91-94825-40282 for the latest details.
How are placements at DSCE Bangalore?
DSCE has a strong placement record with an 80-85% placement rate. Over 150 companies visit the campus for recruitment. The highest package offered is ₹28 LPA, and the average package is ₹5-6 LPA. Top recruiters include Infosys, Accenture, Wipro, TCS, Bosch, Dell, and Cognizant.
Is DSCE affiliated to VTU?
Yes, Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, Karnataka. All degree certificates are awarded by VTU.
Does DSCE have a management quota?
Yes, DSCE has a management quota that constitutes approximately 25% of total seats. Admission through management quota is based on merit in 10+2 and availability of seats. Contact SabkaCollege at +91-94825-40282 for expert guidance on management quota admissions.
What is the campus size of DSCE?
DSCE has a well-equipped 7-acre campus in Kumaraswamy Layout, Bangalore. The campus features smart classrooms, a library with 1,00,000+ books, 18 research centers, hostels, cafeterias, sports grounds, gymnasium, and Wi-Fi connectivity throughout.
Which companies recruit from DSCE?
Top recruiters at DSCE include Infosys, Accenture, Wipro, TCS, Capgemini, Bosch, Dell, and Cognizant among 150+ companies. The placement cell also facilitates internship opportunities with leading organizations.
Does DSCE offer PG programs?
Yes, DSCE offers 6 PG programs including M.Tech in various engineering specializations, MBA (AICTE approved with specializations in Marketing, Finance, HR, and Operations), MCA, and Ph.D. programs. The college also has 18 research centers for Ph.D. studies under VTU. Admission to PG programs is through PGCET, GATE, KMT, CMAT, or MAT depending on the program.
