About RV College of Engineering

Rashtreeya Vidyalaya College of Engineering (RVCE), the flagship institution of Rashtreeya Sikshana Samithi Trust, was established in 1963 on a sprawling 52-acre campus on Mysuru Road, Bangalore. It is one of the top engineering colleges in India, ranked #70 by NIRF, Top 28 by India Today, and 40th best engineering college overall. With excellent academic standards, world-class infrastructure, and outstanding placements, RVCE has earned a stellar reputation among students and recruiters alike.

The institute is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, and operates as an autonomous institution. RVCE holds NAAC A++ accreditation and multiple programs are NBA accredited. It is approved by AICTE, recognized as a nodal center for outcome-based engineering education, and ISTE recognized for its consistent academic excellence.

RVCE offers 15 B.E. specializations including emerging programs such as Aerospace Engineering, CSE (AIML), CSE (Cyber Security), and CSE (Data Science), along with traditional branches like CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, EEE, Chemical, Biotechnology, ETC, ISE, and IEM. The college also offers an MCA program (~₹4,55,000/year, 3-year duration).

Eligibility & Entrance Exams

KCET Quota Eligibility

  • Karnataka domicile with minimum 45% aggregate in PCM (40% for reserved categories)
  • Must have studied in Karnataka for at least 7 years
  • Valid KCET score required for counselling and seat allotment

COMEDK Quota Eligibility

  • Open to all India candidates – no domicile restriction
  • Minimum 45% aggregate in PCM in 10+2 or equivalent
  • Chemistry is compulsory as one of the subjects
  • Valid COMEDK UGET score required

Management Quota Eligibility

  • Minimum 60% in Physics and Mathematics separately in 10+2
  • Must have a valid KCET / COMEDK / JEE rank
  • Admission based on merit and seat availability – no separate counselling required

Frequently Asked Questions

What are the popular courses at RV College of Engineering?
RVCE offers 15 B.E. specializations including CSE, Mechanical, ECE, ISE, Aerospace, CSE(AIML), CSE(Cyber Security), and CSE(Data Science). The college also offers an MCA program (~₹4,55,000/year, 3-year). CSE and ISE are the most sought-after branches with the highest placement packages.
What is the management quota fee at RVCE?
Management quota fees at RVCE vary by branch. CSE is the highest at approximately Rs. 75 Lakhs total for 4 years (Rs. 36L first year + Rs. 13L per year thereafter). Other branches range from Rs. 18 Lakhs (Chemical) to Rs. 67 Lakhs (CSE AIML) total.
What is the COMEDK fee at RVCE?
Under the COMEDK quota, all branches at RVCE have a fee of approximately ₹3.12 Lakh per year. This is uniform across all 15 B.E. specializations offered at the college.
What is the highest package at RVCE?
The highest package at RVCE is ₹67 LPA with an average package of ₹10 LPA. Over 240 companies visit the campus with 1,400 students placed annually. Top recruiters include Microsoft, Google, Amazon, Boeing, Intel, Accenture, Samsung India, and KPMG.
What entrance exams are accepted at RVCE?
RVCE accepts KCET for government quota seats (45%), COMEDK UGET for COMEDK quota seats (30%), and KCET/COMEDK/JEE rank for management quota admission (25%). Contact SabkaCollege at +91-94825-40282 for expert admission guidance.
