About RV College of Engineering

RV College of Engineering (RVCE), established in 1963 on Mysuru Road, Bangalore, is one of the top engineering colleges in India. With excellent academic standards, world-class infrastructure, and outstanding placements, RVCE has earned a stellar reputation among students and recruiters alike.

The institute is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), Belagavi, and operates as an autonomous institution. RVCE holds NAAC A++ accreditation and multiple programs are NBA accredited. It is approved by AICTE and recognized for its consistent academic excellence.

RVCE offers 13 B.E. programs including emerging specializations such as Aerospace Engineering, CSE (AIML), CSE (Cyber Security), and CSE (Data Science), along with traditional branches like CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, EEE, Chemical, Biotechnology, ETC, and IEM.

Eligibility & Entrance Exams

KCET Quota Eligibility

  • Karnataka domicile with minimum 45% aggregate in PCM (40% for reserved categories)
  • Must have studied in Karnataka for at least 7 years
  • Valid KCET score required for counselling and seat allotment

COMEDK Quota Eligibility

  • Open to all India candidates – no domicile restriction
  • Minimum 45% aggregate in PCM in 10+2 or equivalent
  • Chemistry is compulsory as one of the subjects
  • Valid COMEDK UGET score required

Management Quota Eligibility

  • Minimum 60% in Physics and Mathematics separately in 10+2
  • Must have a valid KCET / COMEDK / JEE rank
  • Admission based on merit and seat availability – no separate counselling required

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the fee structure at MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology?
For B.E. programs, KCET students pay approximately ₹1,17,000/year while COMEDK students pay around ₹2,72,000/year. M.Tech and MCA fees are around ₹1,20,000/year and MBA is approximately ₹1,50,000/year. Management quota fees may differ.
What is the placement record at MSRIT?
MSRIT has an excellent placement record with 85-90% placement rate. Over 200 companies visit the campus annually. The average package is ₹6-8 LPA and the highest package recorded is ₹67 LPA. Top recruiters include Amazon, Google, Microsoft, Infosys, and TCS.
What courses are offered at MSRIT Bangalore?
MSRIT offers B.E. programs in 10 branches including CSE, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, EEE, ISE, Chemical, Biotechnology, IEM, and AI&ML. Postgraduate programs include M.Tech (various specializations), MCA, and MBA.
How to get admission in MSRIT?
Admission to MSRIT is through KCET (45% seats), COMEDK UGET (30% seats), and Management quota (25% seats). For B.E., candidates need 45% in PCM with a valid KCET or COMEDK score. Contact SabkaCollege at +91-7676-00-7676 for expert admission guidance.
What is the NIRF ranking of MSRIT?
MS Ramaiah Institute of Technology is ranked #78 in the NIRF (National Institutional Ranking Framework) Engineering category. It is also among the top 10 private engineering colleges in Karnataka.
Does MSRIT have hostel facilities?
Yes, MSRIT provides separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with modern amenities including Wi-Fi, mess facilities, recreation rooms, and 24/7 security. The hostels are located within or near the campus premises.
What is the campus size of MSRIT?
MSRIT has a well-developed 18-acre campus located in Mathikere, Bangalore. The campus houses multiple academic blocks, research labs, libraries, hostels, sports facilities, and a food court with 800+ seating capacity.
Is MSRIT an autonomous college?
Yes, MSRIT is an autonomous institution affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). This means it has the freedom to design its own curriculum, set question papers, and conduct examinations independently while the degree is awarded by VTU.
